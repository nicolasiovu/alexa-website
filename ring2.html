The Second Ring

Here is the ring which you have so graciously gifted me. I wear it every single day without fail. I love being able to look down at my hand and have a constant reminder of the lovely lady which I have the pleasure of knowing. I remember when you told me you got me a ring, I was so thrilled, I was dying with anticipation. And when I finally slipped it onto my finger, it felt amazing. This is a permanent gift, you see. I plan to wear this ring for the rest of my life. Even if a wedding band appears on my hand alongside it, this ring will remain even still. It is forever a reminder of your love.
