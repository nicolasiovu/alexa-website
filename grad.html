Graduation

A rather joyous occasion, especially for me, as I saw you walk along that stage to receive your well deserved award. I was so torn between filming when you went up, or clapping and cheering with all my might. I chose the latter. Thankfully, my parents got the videos, so I got the best of both worlds! I'm happy you liked the flowers, too. Although this day marked the last time I knew for sure I'd see you, which was a little nerve racking, it was still an occasion to celebrate. It all worked out perfectly, too. For your academic achievements, I'm so proud of you. You worked so hard for all that you've attained, and I'm happy to see them come to fruition now. You have only success in your future, I can tell that much.
