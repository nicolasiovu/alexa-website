Merlin #1

I believe now is a good time to address this big penguin that has entered my life mysteriously. Merlin "Merlino" Rigonan is only a few months old, and is the son of an unknown Antarctic married Gentoo penguin couple. Unfortunately, they perished and were unable to take care of their child. An animal rescue company known as Miniso set up an adoption centre for the rescued wildlife. Thankfully, this lady known as Alexa came along and adopted him. And now he is a son to me as well. As we see in this image, his mother loves him very much.
